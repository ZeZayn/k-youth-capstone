Our Story

From One Warehouse to Klang Valley's Trusted Partner

Founded in 2009 with a single warehouse in Klang and 12 staff, LogiMas has grown into one of Klang Valley's most trusted logistics partners. Today we operate 3 warehouses, employ 120 professionals, and handle over 1,000 parcels daily.

In 2024 we began our digital transformation — implementing AI-powered operations, real-time WMS tracking, and an internal AI helpdesk to serve staff and clients better.

Our Journey

2009

Founded in Klang with 1 warehouse and 12 staff members. LogiMas begins its journey.

2012

Opened Shah Alam warehouse and expanded the delivery fleet to serve more clients.

2015

Achieved the milestone of 1,000 daily parcels processed — a landmark for the company.

2018

Launched full WMS (Warehouse Management System) integration across all facilities.

2021

Reached 120 staff members and opened the Subang warehouse for express dispatch operations.

2024

Began AI-powered digital transformation — implementing HELPA AI helpdesk and real-time tracking.
